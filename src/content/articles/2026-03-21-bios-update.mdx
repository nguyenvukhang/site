I'll get straight to the point. I have a [B850M][B850M]. It has
[Q-Flash][qflash]. Here's what I did.

1. Format a USB stick to FAT32/16/12 (as per the Q-Flash website), and plugged
   it into the BIOS-labelled USB port on the motherboard.
2. Download a BIOS version from the [motherboard site][B850M] (look for
   "Support" and "BIOS").
3. Copied and extracted it onto the USB stick.
4. Boot into BIOS.
5. Go to the Q-Flash menu.
6. Select the extracted BIOS file from the USB stick.
7. Update.

The PC then rebooted, but it entered a state of limbo where the motherboard
lights where on (I don't have any other lights in my PC build) but nothing else
seemed to be happening. No screen output and even the fans weren't spinning.
After nervously declaring that I'm in danger and searching the web for a bit, I
decided to cut the power to my PC after 10min, rebooted, and all was well.
