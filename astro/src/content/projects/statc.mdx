StatC is a statistics calculator built for open-book exams, packaged
neatly in a CLI app powered by
[clap.rs](https://docs.rs/clap/latest/clap/#example).

Deployed on [crates.io](https://crates.io/crates/statc), where it has
500+ downloads.
